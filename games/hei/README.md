Earth Invader
=============

Full game is the *earth_invader.py* file

To play:

* w - UP
* a - LEFT
* S - DOWN
* d - RIGHT

To Shoot:
Use space or a mouse click

To win:
kill a total of 50 enemies

To lose:
let the enemies come too close to the spaceship and they will destroy it
